Malpractice insurance pays for losses resulting from injuries to third parties when a professional's conduct falls below the profession's standard of care.

Most people associate malpractice insurance with physicians and surgeons, who are required to have it. However, many others in the medical field also carry malpractice insurance and benefit from coverage.
